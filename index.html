Every pen here at Pen-Tastic is hand turned and created one at a time. No assembly lines here. Our goal is to match the perfect writing instrument with your likes, needs, desires, and price range. With that being said, we have a wide range of truly custom pieces to fit anyone's budget. Our writing instruments start at a reasonable $20 and can go up to $300. Not to mention, if you do not see the perfect pen here on the site for you, you can call us and we will be more than happy to work out the details to build the writing instrument you dream of!

Segmented turning became a passion of mine after seeing my first one. I start with flat lumber, cut it into hundreds of little pieces then glue them together. After that it is on to the lathe. A single piece of wood can have several different colors and grain patterns which all show up in a segmented piece. It is absolutely impossible to have two pieces that are exactly the same. This is what makes them so great for me. After seeing a few of these bowls, vases, and plates I hope you feel the same way.
